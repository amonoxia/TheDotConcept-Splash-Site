Portfolio

  • FosterClub.org
    A National nonprofit organization which is the largest network for youth who have experienced the American foster care system. Website has over 50,000 users and features foster parent online training with youth voice, a discussion forum, resource hub, blogs and more.
  • FamilyFirstAct.org
    As part of the Bipartisan Budget Act (HR. 1892), Family First Act is a law that was passed in 2019. The website is a collaborative project between many child welfare organizations to provide information and resources regarding the law and it's impact.
  • FamilyVoicesUnited.org
    FamilyVoicesUnited.org is a campaign dedicated to ensuring the voices of those with first-hand experience with foster care are heard. Website features a searchable database of stories.
  • SewEducated.org
    SewEducated is an e-commerce website where sponsors can purchase hand-made graduation stoles for foster youth graduating high school.
